Year In Review | 2025

Welcome to our 2024-2025 Year in Review! The Chicago Network is unlike any other organization in the country. We are a powerful community of senior women leaders, all dedicated to one purpose: empowering women to lead.

One of our greatest strengths – and a hallmark of our impact – is our powerful diversity. Our network brings together women from a wide array of industries, backgrounds, and perspectives. This vibrant mix makes us stronger, drives our innovation, and sets us apart as a catalyst for change.

This year, we have elevated our members and their communities in so many ways. We have increased the number of women on boards, expanded the pool of qualified candidates, built new partnerships, and strengthened our visibility locally and globally.

The Chicago Network also continues to serve as a thought leader on women in leadership – sparking conversation, shaping best practices, and influencing progress well beyond our membership. Our voice carries weight, and we work intentionally to move the needle not only in Chicago, but nationally.

One highlight is our Launchpad Women’s Leadership Summit, which offers a truly unique opportunity for emerging women leaders. It is a space for targeted professional development and building a network of peers at similar leadership levels. It embodies our belief in lifting others as you climb – paying it forward so that the next generation of women can thrive.

What makes us truly unique is that everything we do is driven by the passion and strength of our members. We are excited about the future, and we can’t wait to share more with you.

At The Chicago Network, we take immense pride in curating a diverse array of exclusive, high-quality programming that delivers unique value to our members. Our best-in-class programs are intentionally designed to tap into the intellectual curiosity of our community – sparking meaningful conversations, fostering lifelong learning, and encouraging bold ideas. We capitalize on the extraordinary opportunities that Chicago uniquely offers, drawing on the city’s world-class cultural, educational, and research institutions to provide experiences and insights available nowhere else. These programs not only offer members an insider’s perspective on some of Chicago’s most respected organizations but also create powerful forums for connection with other accomplished women.

This past year alone, we proudly hosted over 55 engaging programs, each meticulously designed to inspire, educate, and elevate our members. We are thrilled to share that our members consistently rate The Chicago Network’s programs an impressive 4.9 out of 5, reflecting their exceptional quality and the meaningful impact they have on our community.
