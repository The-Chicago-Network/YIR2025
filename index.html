Year In Review | 2025

Welcome to our 2023-2024 Year in Review! The Chicago Network continues to thrive as we elevate our members and their communities through fostering our purpose, mission, values, and vision. We have much to celebrate as we reflect on our accomplishments this past year.

The Chicago Network's commitment to achieving gender equity remains fundamental to everything we do. Our initiative to increase the number of women on for-profit boards is thriving with more board placements, an expanded number of qualified candidates, increased visibility and recognition as a go-to organization for qualified candidates, new partnerships that boost our reach, robust programming, and enhancements to our womenlead.io database. The Launchpad Women’s Leadership Summit exemplifies our dedication to advancing women in senior leadership roles. It provides high potential women tailored professional growth experiences and facilitates peer connections across diverse industries.

Our members are our lifeline and what makes us an exceptional organization. We listen to them, assist them in achieving their goals, support their personal growth, and are grateful for them.
